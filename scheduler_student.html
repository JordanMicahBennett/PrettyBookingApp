's Booking Guide



You may book by clicking the black paper plane icon in the time schedule.

Black paper planes in schedule are unbooked items,
while yellow paper plane items represent those that have already been booked.

You may filter the table by dates, using the black pencil icon atop time schedule,
to see booked times across different months, and different days.

You may also filter the table by driver schedule, using the black animated wrench icon atop time schedule,
to see availabilities based on driver.

Finally, take a look at our list of driver/vehicle combinations, in the drivers tab, to the left.
